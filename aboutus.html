Our Story

Wheel Hub isn't just a car dealership, it's a legacy of connecting people with their perfect ride. Since 1995, we've been a trusted name in automotive retail across the United States, building a reputation for quality cars and exceptional service. Now, we're bringing that same dedication to the online world. We understand that car buying can be overwhelming, but at Wheel Hub, we want to change that. Our expansive selection – from sleek sedans to rugged SUVs – is curated to fit your lifestyle, not just your budget. We believe the car buying journey should be seamless and exciting. That's why we've created a platform that allows you to explore, compare, and confidently purchase your next car, all from the comfort of your own home. We're not just selling cars, we're curating your journey. Welcome to the future of car buying, welcome to Wheel Hub.

Our Mission and Vision

The Mission

At Wheel Hub, our mission is to revolutionize the automotive retail experience by providing a diverse selection of high-quality vehicles and accessories, exceptional customer service, and a seamless, enjoyable buying process both in our showrooms and online. We are dedicated to offering options that meet the unique needs of every driver, creating a welcoming and informative environment where customers feel valued and supported. Embracing innovation, our user-friendly online platform allows for easy exploration, comparison, and purchase of vehicles. Committed to integrity, trust, and sustainability, we aim to build lasting relationships and make a positive impact on our communities and the environment.

The Vision

At Wheel Hub, our vision is to lead the automotive retail industry by seamlessly integrating cutting-edge technology with exceptional customer service, creating a world where buying a car is as enjoyable and effortless as driving one. We aspire to set new standards in quality and innovation, offering an extensive range of vehicles and accessories that cater to every lifestyle and preference. Our commitment to sustainability and community engagement will drive us to make meaningful contributions to the environment and the communities we serve. Through trust, integrity, and continuous improvement, we envision a future where Wheel Hub is synonymous with excellence and the ultimate car buying experience.
